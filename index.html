AI Study Assistant

Empowering students through intelligent study

Problem & Opportunity

Students often struggle with time management and procrastination. Without personalized guidance they miss deadlines, feel stressed, and their grades suffer. This presents a big opportunity to help learners improve their study habits and achieve better results.

许多学生缺乏时间管理，经常拖延且缺乏个性化指导，这导致错过截止日期、压力增加和成绩下降。这也为帮助学习者改善学习习惯、取得更好结果提供了机遇。

Our Solution

AI Study Assistant creates a personalized study plan and schedule for each learner. It sends focus reminders, blocks distractions, and provides fun rewards and adaptive challenges to keep learners engaged and motivated.

AI 助学助手为每个学习者制定个性化学习计划和日程。它会发送专注提醒，阻止分心，并通过有趣的奖励和自适应挑战让学习者保持投入和动力。

Technology

Our assistant uses machine-learning models to analyse study habits and update plans in real time. Natural language processing helps summarise and explain course material, while adaptive algorithms adjust schedules based on each learner’s progress.

助手利用机器学习分析学习习惯并实时更新计划。自然语言处理帮助总结并解释学习材料，自适应算法根据每位学习者的进度调整日程。

Business Model

Core features are free for everyone. A paid upgrade unlocks advanced features and detailed analytics. We also license the assistant to educational institutions and support limited advertising in the free version.

核心功能对所有人免费。付费升级可以解锁高级功能和详细分析。我们还向教育机构授权，并在免费版中支持有限广告。

Go-To-Market

We will launch on major app stores, partner with universities and schools, collaborate with influencers, run a referral program, and create helpful online content to reach learners worldwide.

我们将在主要应用商店上线，与大学和学校合作，联手影响者开展推广，运行推荐奖励计划，并制作有用的线上内容以触及全球学习者。

Competitive Advantage

Unlike single-purpose study apps, AI Study Assistant combines personalised scheduling, focus support, and gamification into one holistic tool.

与只提供单一功能的学习应用不同，AI 助学助手将个性化日程、专注支持和游戏化激励整合在一款工具中。

Management Team

我们的团队包括一位 AI 研究员创始人、一位注重用户体验的产品经理、几位大学教授和教育科技顾问，以及由工程师和设计师组成的技术团队。

Our Vision

With strong user growth and sustainable revenue, our vision is to empower learners around the world to study efficiently and enjoyably.

随着用户增长和持续收入的实现，我们的愿景是让全球学习者高效、愉快地学习。
